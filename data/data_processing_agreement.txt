DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT (DPA)

This Data Processing Agreement (“DPA”) is entered into between Acme Corp (“Controller”) and Vendor XYZ Pvt. Ltd. (“Processor”).

1. Scope and Purpose

Processor shall process personal data solely for providing services under the Vendor Services Agreement.

2. Data Security

Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect personal data.

3. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach:

Processor shall notify Controller without undue delay

In any case, notification shall occur within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach

4. Subprocessors

Processor may engage subprocessors with prior written authorization.

5. Liability

The DPA does not specify an independent liability cap and relies on the Vendor Services Agreement.

6. Governing Law

This DPA shall be governed by the laws of the European Union (GDPR) and, where applicable, local member state law.
