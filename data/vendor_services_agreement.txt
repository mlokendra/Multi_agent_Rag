VENDOR SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Vendor Services Agreement (“Agreement”) is made between Acme Corp and Vendor XYZ Pvt. Ltd.

Vendor shall provide software development and maintenance services as described in Schedule A.

2. Payment Terms

Acme Corp shall pay Vendor a fixed monthly fee. Late payments may incur interest.

3. Indemnification

Vendor agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Acme Corp from any third-party claims arising from:

Intellectual property infringement

Vendor’s negligence or willful misconduct

4. Limitation of Liability

Except for indemnification obligations and gross negligence:

Each party’s total liability shall not exceed the total fees paid in the preceding 12 months

Either party may terminate:

For convenience, with 60 days written notice

Immediately, in case of material breach not cured within 15 days

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
