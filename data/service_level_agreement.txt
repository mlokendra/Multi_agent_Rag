SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)

This Service Level Agreement (“SLA”) forms part of the Vendor Services Agreement.

1. Service Availability

Vendor shall ensure 99.5% monthly uptime.

2. Service Credits

If uptime falls below the agreed threshold:

Vendor shall provide service credits as defined in Schedule B

Service credits are the sole and exclusive remedy

3. Exclusions

Downtime caused by:

Scheduled maintenance

Force majeure events

Customer misconfiguration
shall be excluded from uptime calculations.

4. Liability

Vendor shall not be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages.
